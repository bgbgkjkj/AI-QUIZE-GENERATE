# User Profile Picture - Quick Guide

## How to Upload Your Profile Picture

### Step 1: Go to Your Profile
- Click your profile icon in the top-right navigation menu
- Select "View Profile" from the dropdown
- Or navigate directly to the Profile page

### Step 2: Edit Your Profile Picture
- Click the **camera icon** on your profile avatar (blue circle with your initials)
- The icon appears in the bottom-left corner of your avatar

### Step 3: Select an Image
- A modal dialog will open titled "Edit Profile Picture"
- Click **"Choose New Picture"** button
- Select a JPG or PNG image from your computer
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Your selected image will appear as a preview in the modal

### Step 4: Save Your Changes
- Review your preview image
- Click the **"Save"** button to upload
- Wait for the upload to complete (shows "Saving..." during upload)
- Your profile picture will be updated across the application

### Step 5: View Your Picture
Your profile picture will now appear in:
- ✅ Your profile page avatar
- ✅ Navigation menu profile button (top-right)
- ✅ Leaderboard rankings (if in top 10)
- ✅ Other pages where user profiles are displayed

## Supported Image Formats
- **JPG/JPEG** - Recommended, best compression
- **PNG** - Best for transparent backgrounds
- Maximum size: **5MB**

## What Happens After Upload?

1. **Storage** - Your picture is stored securely on the server
2. **Display** - Picture appears immediately in your profile
3. **Leaderboard** - Shows on leaderboard if you're in top 10
4. **Persistence** - Stays with your account permanently

## Troubleshooting

### Image Not Uploading?
- Check file size (max 5MB)
- Ensure file format is JPG or PNG
- Check your internet connection
- Look for error messages in the modal

### Image Not Displaying?
- Refresh the page (F5 or Ctrl+R)
- Clear browser cache
- Check if file was actually uploaded by trying again
- Contact support if issue persists

### Want to Change Your Picture?
- Simply follow the steps above again with a new image
- Old picture will be replaced automatically

## Privacy Note
- Profile pictures are visible to other users
- Only shown if your profile is set to "Public"
- You can control visibility in Settings page

## Tips for Best Results
1. Use a clear, well-lit photo
2. Square images work best (profile pictures are circular)
3. Avoid images larger than 5MB to reduce upload time
4. JPG format for photographs, PNG for graphics
5. Keep resolution at least 200x200 pixels
